Therapist, clinical supervisor, speaker, & consultant.

About Me

Hi, I’m Leila Anderson, LMFT-S, a therapist with over a decade of experience helping individuals navigate mental health challenges, addiction, and the complexities of modern life. I specialize in working with spiritual trauma, complicated grief, and shame, helping clients untangle the deeply held beliefs that keep them feeling stuck.

I know firsthand how powerful, challenging, and painful wrestling with old beliefs and values can be. I was raised in religious spaces filled both with love and unspoken fear and shame. Rigid, black-and-white thinking can make the world feel safe only when it stays small, profoundly limiting our understanding of ourselves and the world. Healing means making space for curiosity, nuance, and self-trust. My approach blends evidence-based therapy with deep compassion, helping you reconnect with your inner wisdom and build a life that feels authentic and free.

When I’m not working, I’m a proud mom of three, married to my favorite hiking partner, and always up for an adventure—whether it’s tackling a challenging puzzle, nurturing my garden, or exploring Austin’s trails. If you ever need coffee recommendations, I’ve got you covered after five years as a barista perfecting my love for the perfect cup (hint: Chemex and Ethiopian medium roast - great place to start!).

Feel free to reach out with any questions. I’d love to hear from you!

Therapy

I offer trauma-informed, client-centered therapy to help you reconnect with your authentic self and lead a life guided by joy and purpose.

Clinical Supervision

As an AAMFT and Texas Approved LMFT Supervisor, I provide supportive, growth-focused licensure supervision to help LMFT Associates refine their skills and deepen their confidence in clinical work.

Consultation

I provide clinical consultation and training to individuals and organizations on topics like neurodivergence, spiritual trauma, and shame, empowering teams to better serve diverse client needs.
