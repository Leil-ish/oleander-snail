Hi, I’m Leila Anderson, LMFT-S.

Your guide for coming home to yourself.
As a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, Clinical Supervisor, Consultant, and Speaker, I blend rigorous clinical expertise with a deeply human, shame-reducing stance. Rooted in justice and powered by presence, my work is about helping you integrate your intellect and emotion, your past and your powerful present.

You're Self-Aware. Driven. And Perhaps, Deeply Exhausted.

You've navigated complex systems—family, faith, culture—that tried to define or diminish you, and you're ready to unravel the internalized shame they left behind.

Your high-functioning exterior often masks cycles of self-criticism and over-functioning, leaving you feeling disconnected from your true self.

You crave a therapeutic space that honors your intellect and your emotional world, rather than pathologizing your protective strategies.

You're tired of quick fixes and ready for a depth-oriented approach that helps you integrate your mind, body, and story.

You know there's more to life than survival mode, and you're ready to live in alignment with your values and full humanity.

Where Clinical Expertise Meets Lived Wisdom.

My approach is distinct:

I'm not afraid to name the invisible forces—family systems, cultural systems, colonial systems—that have shaped your story, helping you see how you've internalized their impact.

We move beyond analysis, using metaphor, movement, and meaning-making to help you live into new narratives, not just dissect old ones.

Drawing on diverse lived experiences across cultures, faith traditions, and roles, I hold the complexity of your story without collapsing into binaries, creating a space where you feel truly accompanied, not managed.

For practices and leaders, I translate clinical nuance into strategic insight, building programs and curriculums that are trauma-literate, neurodivergent-affirming, ethical, and equitable.
