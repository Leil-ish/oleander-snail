How We Work Together

Therapy that Liberates.

                               

This isn't just about coping; it's about reclaiming. We offer trauma-informed, transformative care across Texas (including Austin), specializing in unraveling spiritual trauma, complex grief, and the deep roots of shame. Through modalities like EMDR, DBT, and IFS, delivered with a relational, system-aware lens, we’ll help you process, integrate, and finally thrive. Whether you're navigating neurodivergence or exploring identity in nuanced ways, this space is for your full humanity.

Fee: $200 per hour. Superbills and out-of-network reimbursement supported via Mentaya. Sliding scale options are available during a free consultation.

Hi, I’m Leila Anderson, LMFT-S.

Think of me as your trusted guide for un-shrinking your life and leadership. I'm a therapist, supervisor, consultant, and speaker fiercely rooted in justice and powered by presence. I'm here to help you shed what no longer serves you and truly come home to your most authentic, powerful self. You're not broken. You make profound sense—and it's time you fully owned that truth.

My dedication to this work isn't just professional; it's deeply personal. I was raised in religious spaces filled with both love and unspoken fear. That experience profoundly shaped my passion for helping others untangle old beliefs and build a life led by curiosity, nuance, and self-trust.

Where Clinical Expertise Meets Lived Wisdom

I bring over a decade of experience across diverse clinical, academic, and leadership settings. My education includes an M.A. in Marriage and Family Therapy from Abilene Christian University and a B.A. in Psycholinguistics from Harding University.

Beyond credentials, my approach is distinct: I blend rigorous clinical knowledge (EMDR, DBT, IFS) with a deeply relational, shame-reducing stance. I'm not afraid to name systems—family systems, cultural systems, colonial systems—and help you see how they've shaped you. My past roles, including Owner of Oleander Counseling and Consulting, Clinical Strategist at Lucent Recovery and Wellness, and Executive Clinical Director at Ava Recovery Center, among others, have given me a unique perspective. I'm also a proud Board Member of the Texas Association for MFT and a presenter at various professional conferences. I draw on lived experience across cultures, faith traditions, and roles (mother, clinician, advocate), which helps me hold complexity without collapsing into binaries.
