Hi, I’m Leila Anderson, LMFT-S.

Therapist. Supervisor. Consultant. Speaker.
Rooted in justice. Powered by presence.
Here to help you come home to yourself.

You are not broken. You make sense.

Maybe you're carrying spiritual trauma no one knows about. Maybe you're constantly over-functioning to silence shame. Maybe you're just tired of therapy that pathologizes instead of humanizes.

I work with adults — especially women and queer folks — who’ve survived systems that tried to shrink them. You're self-aware, high-achieving, and likely exhausted.

At Oleander, you’ll find a space where intellect and emotion, body and story, grief and joy are all welcome.

Therapy

In-person and online trauma-informed care across Texas, specializing in spiritual trauma, complex grief, and shame. EMDR, DBT, and IFS offered through a relational, system-aware lens.

Clinical Supervision

Supportive, equity-rooted supervision for LMFT Associates. Relational, rigorous, and designed to help you trust your voice as a clinician.

Consulting & Speaking

Training, curriculum, and strategic insight for practices, programs, and leaders seeking trauma-literate, justice-aligned transformation.

Signature Trainings & Presentations

All Behavior Makes Sense in Context

Explore how complex trauma shapes behavior often misread as personality pathology — with reflections on the politics of diagnosis.

Understand how religious systems create trauma and how to respond with clinical integrity and deep compassion.

The Interpersonal Self

Integrate DBT and IFS to support clients navigating trauma-influenced emotional and relational dysregulation.

Connecting Like Elmer’s

Build non-toxic leadership grounded in ethics, humility, and relational accountability — no glue gun required.
