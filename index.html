Healing and Transformation

I'm Leila Anderson, LMFT-S, a therapist committed to helping individuals overcome the impact of complex trauma, religious trauma, shame, and grief. Through evidence-based therapies like EMDR, IFS, SFBT, DBT, and experiential approaches, I guide you on a journey towards healing, empowerment, and a life lived authentically.

About Me

With over a decade of experience, I've had the privilege of working with diverse individuals and families. My approach is grounded in empathy, compassion, and a deep understanding of the human experience. I believe in creating a safe and supportive space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors without judgment.

Together, we'll work to uncover the root causes of your challenges, develop healthy coping mechanisms, and build resilience. My goal is to empower you to live a fulfilling life, free from the constraints of the past.

My Approach

I utilize a variety of therapeutic techniques tailored to your unique needs. Here are some of the modalities I employ:

  • EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing): A powerful technique for processing traumatic memories and reducing their emotional impact.
  • IFS (Internal Family Systems): A transformative approach that helps you identify and heal the parts of yourself that may be causing distress.
  • SFBT (Solution-Focused Brief Therapy): A future-oriented therapy that focuses on strengths and solutions to achieve positive change.
  • DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy): A comprehensive approach that teaches skills for emotional regulation, distress tolerance, interpersonal effectiveness, and mindfulness.
  • Experiential Therapies: A variety of techniques that emphasize direct experience and personal insight.

By combining these approaches, we can work together to address your specific concerns and develop personalized strategies for healing and growth.
