I am Leila Anderson

Therapist, clinical supervisor, speaker, & consultant.

Coffee Facts:

I brew with a Chemex and drink my coffee black, but at one time, I was the latte art champion of my coffee shop. I love an Ethiopian medium roast. I'm so particular about the temperature of my coffee that I can hardly use travel mugs: If I'm walking around with my coffee and it hits the perfect temperature, I have to stop and drink it all right then. I was late for college classes a non-zero number of times because of this. I am a firm believer in the power of sharing a cup of coffee (or tea) in forming connections with folks. I'll never have a therapy office without hot drink options!

Life in Austin

I ended up in Austin randomly - the first job I got after graduate school was here. Despite roasting through every summer, I've never left. I love the range of outdoor activities that Austin offers - being in nature is good for the brain and good for the body.

About Me

Hi, I’m Leila Anderson, LMFT-S, LCDC—a therapist with over a decade of experience helping individuals navigate mental health challenges, addiction, and the complexities of modern life. My career has taken me from hospitals and treatment centers to private practice and correctional facilities, always focused on creating space for healing and transformation.

I blend innovative therapy techniques with timeless practices that foster meaningful, lasting change. I specialize in treating spiritual trauma, complicated grief, and shame, approaching each session with compassion, curiosity, and a commitment to my clients’ growth. Growing up in Uganda, I was shaped by a rich tapestry of cultures and experiences, which inform my deep respect for the intersection of identity, community, and healing.

When I’m not working, I’m a proud mom of three, married to my favorite hiking partner, and always up for an adventure—whether it’s tackling a challenging puzzle, nurturing my garden, or exploring Austin’s trails. If you ever need coffee recommendations, I’ve got you covered after five years as a barista perfecting my love for the perfect cup.

Feel free to reach out with any questions. I’d love to hear from you!

Therapy

I offer trauma-informed, client-centered therapy to help you reconnect with your authentic self and lead a life guided by joy and purpose.

Clinical Supervision

As an AAMFT and Texas Approved LMFT Supervisor, I provide supportive, growth-focused licensure supervision to help LMFT Associates refine their skills and deepen their confidence in clinical work.

Consultation

I provide clinical consultation and training to individuals and organizations on topics like neurodivergence, spiritual trauma, and shame, empowering teams to better serve diverse client needs.
